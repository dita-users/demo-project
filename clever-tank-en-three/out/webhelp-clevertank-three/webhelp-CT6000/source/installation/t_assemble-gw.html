Assembling group 1

Please refer to the technical data sheet for all connecting dimensions.

  1. Locate the grey water inflow from the building.
  2. Connect the inflow.

    Venting must be ensured via the inflow.

  3. Connect the overflow.

    The grey water overflow must be provided with an on-site connection to the existing wastewater line.

    Please ensure an adequate backflow device in the overflow line.

  4. Connect the tubes of the filtration pump to the nozzles of assembly group 2 (observe designation/colour coding).

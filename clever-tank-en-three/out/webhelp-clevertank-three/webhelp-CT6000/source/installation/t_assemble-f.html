Assembling group 2

  1. Connect the grey water filtration overflow to the on-site wastewater line.
  2. Connect vent.

    In addition, the assembly group with tank 1 must be vented via an accompanying connection to assembly group 2.

  3. Connect the tube of the feed pump.
  4. The tubes of the filtration pump must be connected to the nozzles of assembly group 3, provided for that purpose (observe designation/colour coding).

Assembling group 2

The transfer point of service water is mounted on this tank. Eventually, an external pressure booster unit can be connected here.

Please apply all connecting dimensions according to the technical data sheet.

  1. The overflow of the service water group must also be connected on-site to the existing wastewater line.
  2. In order to ensure the supply to the service water network, the potable water backfeed must be connected on-site by means of a pressure line.
  3. Connect the tube of the filtration pump of assembly group 1.

Product description

The systems are intended for multi-stage treatment of grey water and provision of service water. The system comprises multiple assembly groups.

  • Group 1: Collect and purify grey water

    In the first assembly group, grey water is collected and purified.

  • Group 2: Grey water filtration

    The second assembly group is for grey water filtration with the CleverTank membrane modules.

  • Group 3: Service water storage

    In this assembly group the filtered grey water is stored as service water. In addition, the last tank has a potable water feed in order to ensure the supply of service water to the network.

  • Group 4: Control system

    CleverTank grey water systems are equipped with a fourth assembly group, a fully automatic control system for regulating and monitoring. It is provided with a potential-free contact to integrate fault indications in the building management system.

Tank volumes can vary according to the type of system.
